We handle everything in-house — artwork preparation, colour profiling, printing and installation. Our materials are weatherproof, UV-stable and engineered to maintain their impact for years in the Irish climate.

In-House Production

From artwork to finished installation — everything happens under our roof. No outsourcing means tighter quality control and faster turnaround times for your project.

Weather & UV Resistant

Any Scale

From a single A3 decal to a multi-storey building wrap — our print capability and installation expertise scales to any project requirement.

Spaces & Architecture

Intelligent Film
for Intelligent Spaces.

Architectural window film and surface wrap solutions offer a transformative yet non-destructive way to update interior and exterior spaces. From creating frosted privacy screens to applying branded decorative film, WTS Ireland works with architects, designers and businesses to realise their vision.

Our architectural film range spans solar control, privacy, decorative, safety and branded applications — each specified to balance aesthetics, performance and longevity in your specific environment.

Non-Destructive

All film applications are fully reversible with no damage to underlying surfaces — ideal for leased commercial spaces where permanent modifications aren't permitted.

Architect & Designer Friendly

We work collaboratively with architects and interior designers to specify the right film for each application, providing samples, CAD templates and technical data sheets.

Film Applications

Frosted & Privacy Film

Create private meeting rooms, partition screens and bathroom glazing with a range of frosted, etched and gradient privacy films.

Solar Control Film

Reduce solar heat gain and glare in glazed commercial spaces while maintaining natural light and outward visibility. Lowers energy costs significantly.

Decorative & Branded Film

Apply custom patterned, coloured or branded film to glass, walls and surfaces to reinforce your identity throughout your space.

Safety & Security Film

Optically clear safety film holds glass together in impact, significantly reducing injury risk from shattered glazing in retail or public spaces.

Anti-Graffiti Film

Sacrificial anti-graffiti film protects glazing and cladding from vandalism — remove and replace rather than repair or replace the surface beneath.

One-Way Mirror Film

Create a mirrored exterior effect from outside while maintaining clear visibility from within — ideal for offices, retail and hospitality environments.

Exterior Cladding Wrap

Transform building exteriors and architectural cladding with cast vinyl wraps — a cost-effective alternative to full cladding replacement or repainting.

Surface & Furniture Wrap

Update furniture, counter tops, cabinetry and interior surfaces with premium surface films — available in wood, stone, metal and solid colour finishes.
